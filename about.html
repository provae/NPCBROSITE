FAQ

Frequently asked questions about NPCBRO.

What is NPCBRO?

NPCBRO is a memecoin project on Solana featuring a character who escaped the script. It's a community-driven project focused on gaming, AI, and digital experiences.

When is the launch?

The official launch is scheduled for August 1, 2026. Join our waitlist to get notified when we go live.

How can I buy NPCBRO?

After launch, you can buy NPCBRO on decentralized exchanges like Jupiter or Raydium using your Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.).

What makes NPCBRO different?

NPCBRO combines gaming, AI, and community into one universe. We're building actual experiences, not just another token.

Is there a team?

Yes, we have a dedicated team working on development, community management, and future expansions.

How can I join the community?

Follow us on Twitter and join our Discord. Early community members get exclusive access to features and airdrops.
